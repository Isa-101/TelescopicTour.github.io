TelescopicTour

TelescopicTour is an American aerospace company dedicated to revolutionizing the aerospace industry and making affordable spaceflight a reality. Our company was founded with the goal of making the excitement and wonder of space accessible to anyone with the desire to explore. With TelescopicTour, you can choose from a variety of space-based travel packages, including suborbital “hot-air balloon” flights, rocket launches, and visits to the International Space Station. We are committed to ensuring the safety and comfort of all of our guests and have implemented rigorous training and safety protocols to ensure that every mission is a success. Whether you are a seasoned space enthusiast or just looking for a once-in-a-lifetime adventure, TelescopicTour has something for you. We invite you to join us on this incredible journey of discovery and exploration.

About Safety

At TelescopicTour, the safety of our passengers is our top priority. All of our spacecrafts are rigorously tested and meet the highest industry safety standards. Our pilots have received comprehensive safety training and are experienced professionals. In the unlikely event of an emergency, our spacecrafts are equipped with the latest technology to ensure the safest possible outcome for all passengers and crew on board. We also have detailed emergency procedures in place and conduct regular drills to ensure that our crew is prepared for any situation. At TelescopicTour, we take every precaution to ensure that your journey with us is as safe and enjoyable as possible.

Safety Regulations

Due to safety concerns, All TelescopicTour Astronauts must meet the safety regulations put in place by the industry. Astronauts must Measure between 149.5 cm and 190.5 cm (4’10 to 6’3), weigh between 50 and 95 kilograms (110 and 209 pounds).

Explore Training

At TelescopicTour, the safety and comfort of our passengers is of the utmost importance. That's why we have a thorough and rigorous training program in place for all of our staff, including our pilots, and ground crew. Our pilots undergo extensive training in order to be able to safely operate our spacecraft and navigate the challenges of space travel. This includes both classroom instruction and hands-on training, as well as simulated flights and emergency drills. In addition, they must pass rigorous physical and psychological evaluations to ensure that they are fit for the demands of space travel. Our ground crew undergoes training in order to ensure the smooth and efficient operation of our spacecraft, both before and after flights. This includes everything from preparing the spacecraft for launch to conducting post-flight inspections and maintenance.
